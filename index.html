Getting started

Boxing is undoubtedly one of the oldest sports in the world. In the beginning, it was known as pugilism or pancracking. Works of art from the Sumerian and Egyptian civilisations, dating back to more than 3000 BC, show fight scenes that prove that boxing has existed ever since. The sport has obviously evolved considerably since then. Although English boxing is the leading discipline, there are many extensions.
